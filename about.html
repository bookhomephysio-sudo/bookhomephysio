Our Mission

Physiotherapy should come to the patient — not the other way around.

Millions of patients across India skip physiotherapy because of transport barriers, clinic queues, and the exhaustion of travelling while in pain. We built BookHomePhysio to eliminate every one of those barriers.

Better outcomes. Less friction.
