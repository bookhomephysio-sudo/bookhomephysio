FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can a therapist arrive? ▾
Same-day bookings: average 2–4 hours. Next-day bookings are almost always fulfilled.
Is the ₹200 token refundable? ▾
Refundable if cancelled 4+ hours before session. Within 4 hours: non-refundable but we can reschedule.
Are therapists certified? ▾
Yes — every therapist holds a BPT or MPT degree from a recognised institution, verified before first dispatch.
What equipment do they bring? ▾
TENS/EMS units, ultrasound therapy, resistance bands, massage tools, and all consumables needed.
Can I request the same therapist? ▾
Yes — package bookings include a dedicated therapist. For single sessions, request via WhatsApp.
How do I pay the balance? ▾
Pay ₹1,000 directly to the therapist after session via cash or UPI.
Do you serve corporate clients? ▾
Yes — bulk pricing, account manager, monthly reports. Email bookhomephysio@gmail.com for a quote.
What if I'm unhappy with my session? ▾
Rate 3★ or below and our admin is immediately alerted to contact you privately before any public review.
